About

Built for the operational reality of fleet & fintech CX

I sit where customer experience, fintech products, and logistics operations meet. At BlackBuck, that means managing a live portfolio of B2B transport companies and B2C commercial vehicle operators — handling onboarding tickets, FASTag KYC, renewals, and escalations every single day, while also being judged on hard acquisition and cross-sell numbers.

It's a dual-role grind: part support desk, part sales engine. I've stayed in it for 5+ years because I'm good at the part most people find hardest — keeping a fleet owner calm during an escalation, then turning that same relationship into a GPS or Loan upsell three months later.

Recognised with a Mission Sparta Bronze Award and an 'Exceeds Expectation' rating — not for one big win, but for consistency across renewals, activations, and retention, quarter after quarter.
